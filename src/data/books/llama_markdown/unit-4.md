**A Holiday Plan**

**Shasha:** It'll be the school holiday soon! Where shall we go?
**Dad:** Let's go to Yan'an.
**Shasha:** How will we get there?
**Mum:** We'll take the high-speed train.
**Jinjin:** I love trains! What will we do there?
**Dad:** We can explore Yan'an Revolutionary Memorial Hall. We'll visit the pagoda on Pagoda Hill. We'll take a bus to visit the Hukou Waterfall. It'll take us about three hours.
**Shasha:** Where will we stay in Yan'an?
**Mum:** We'll stay in a cave house!
**Jinjin:** Wow! Will there be good food?
**Dad:** Yan'an is famous for deep-fried cakes.
**Shasha:** It sounds great. I can't wait to go!

Welcome to Yan'an

About Yan'an

Yan'an is in Shaanxi Province. It is a wonderful city.

Things to do

- See the views on Pagoda Hill
- Learn about China's history at Yan'an Revolutionary Memorial Hall
- See the Hukou Waterfall
- Stay in a cave house
- Enjoy deep-fried cakes on the Yellow River

**Holidays up high**

Mountains are tall and beautiful. Many people like to visit them on their holidays. Here are three amazing mountains.

**Mount Taishan**

Mount Taishan is a wonderful mountain in China. Visitors can see caves and waterfalls. The tallest peak is Jade Emperor Peak. Visitors climb it to watch the sunrise.

---

Mont Blanc
Mont Blanc is in France and Italy. In summer, visitors hike in the mountains; in winter, visitors go skiing on the snow.

Mount Qomolangma
Mount Qomolangma is in China and Nepal. It is the world's tallest mountain. It is very hard to climb.

Would you like to spend your holidays in these mountains?
