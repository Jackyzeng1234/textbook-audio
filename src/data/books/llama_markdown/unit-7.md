**New Year's Day around the world**

It will be New Year's Day in two days. How will you celebrate it? Let's find out how people around the world celebrate the New Year. It is always fun and exciting!

**China**
In China, we celebrate New Year's Day with our family and friends. We relax and have fun. We hope to have a good year.

**USA**
In the USA, people watch fireworks on New Year's Eve. On New Year's Day, many people watch American football matches.

In Scotland, people visit their family and friends. They bring a piece of coal for good luck.
