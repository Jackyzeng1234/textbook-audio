Living and

We see many things in our world every day. All these things are living or non-living. How are they different?

Living things
A living thing can grow and change. A plant is a living thing. An animal is a living thing. You are a living thing! Living things need air, food, and water. People and animals can move. They can get food and water from different places. Plants cannot move by themselves. They make their food from sunlight, water, and air. What living things do you see every day?
