**Life in different countries**

To Adam
Subject: Life in Chengdu

Hi!
My name is Li Wei. I'm 12 years old. I'm from China.
I live in Chengdu. It's in the southwest of China. There are lots of things to do here. We like going to the teahouses. We sometimes go to see the pandas at the panda base. We love the cute pandas.
I'm in Grade Five. There's a Sichuan Opera club in our school. I learn Sichuan Opera in the club after school. My sister and I love hot pot.
Please write and tell me about yourself.
Yours,
Li Wei
