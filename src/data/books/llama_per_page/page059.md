**Extend**

**Living to non-living**

Some living things can become non-living things. Is the boy's straw hat from a living thing?

Wheat grows in fields.

Farmers cut the wheat.

It becomes straw. The sun dries the straw.

People can make a hat with the straw.

Straw begins as a living thing: wheat. People make it into a hat. The wheat becomes a non-living thing.
