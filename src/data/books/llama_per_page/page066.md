**How We Hurt Our Natural Resources**

We should not hurt our natural resources; we need them to live. Smoke from cars and factories makes the air dirty. Dirty air can make people sick. Rubbish makes the land and water dirty. We cannot drink dirty water, and we cannot use dirty land.

**How We Should Take Care of Our Natural Resources**

We should all take care of the Earth's natural resources. Here are some ideas:
- Use things longer.
- Reduce the things we use.
- Recycle our rubbish.
