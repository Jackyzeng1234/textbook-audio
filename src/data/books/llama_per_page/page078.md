MERRY CHRISTMAS

To: LiNa
Subject: Christmas

Dear Li Na,
The Spring Festival sounds fun! In the UK, our main festival is Christmas. About one month before Christmas, we put up a Christmas tree at home. On Christmas Eve, I always put a stocking by my bed. The next morning, there are gifts in it! My grandparents come on Christmas Day. First, we open our gifts. Then, we eat a big meal.

Yours,
Katie
