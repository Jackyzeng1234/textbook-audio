To Katie
Subject: Spring Festival

Dear Katie,
I'm very excited! It'll be the Spring Festival next month. We'll clean our house. This is hard work, but it'll bring good luck. I'll help my grandmother make jiaozi; it's my favorite food. Then I'll watch a lion dance with my family. And we'll visit my aunt and uncle. We'll have a big meal together. What festivals do you celebrate?

Yours,
Li Na

This is HARD WORK, but it'll bring GOOD LUCK.
