**Non-living Things**

A non-living thing does not need air, food, or water. A toy is a non-living thing. A non-living thing does not grow, but it can change. Look at the shoes. How are they different?

A ball can move. Is it a living thing? No, it isn't. It cannot move by itself. It does not grow. Look around you. What non-living things do you see?

All living and non-living things in our world are important. We should take care of them.
