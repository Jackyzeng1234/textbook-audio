A holiday plan
Shasha: It'll be the school holiday soon! Where shall we go?
Dad: Let's go to Yan'an.
Shasha: How will we get there?
Mum: We'll take the high-speed train.
Jinjin: I love trains! What will we do there?
Dad: We can explore Yan'an Revolutionary Memorial Hall. We'll visit the pagoda on Pagoda Hill. We'll take a bus to visit the Hukou Waterfall. It'll take us about three hours.
Shasha: Where will we stay in Yan'an?
Mum: We'll stay in a cave house!
Jinjin: Wow! Will there be good food?
Dad: Yan'an is famous for deep-fried cakes.
Shasha: It sounds great. I can't wait to go!
