**My Interview**
Me: Where will you go for holiday, Grandma?
Grandma: Your grandpa and I will go to the country.
Me: Sounds good. What will you do there?
Grandma: We'll do exercise every morning. Your grandpa will go fishing and I'll take photos with my friends. Of course, we'll eat a lot of healthy food.
Me: Great! And how long will you stay there?
Grandma: About two weeks.
