**Listen and Chant.**

**The Earth**

All the land and all the sea -
Everything you see... is me!
The air and wood and water too
Are things that I give to you.
You should recycle and keep me clean.
You shouldn't cut down trees, keep me green.

**Learn**
We should recycle things.
should not → shouldn't
We should not hurt our natural resources.
